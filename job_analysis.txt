JOB MATCH ANALYSIS
==============================

1. ATS KEYWORDS
- IT Support Specialist
- user account administration

- technical support

2. TECHNICAL SKILLS REQUIRED
- User account administration (network, email, applications)
- Troubleshooting computer hardware and peripherals
- Experience with Office 365 and cloud-based applications
- Knowledge of Exchange Server Tools
- Familiarity with IT ticketing systems
- Operating systems support
- Experience with building security and environment monitoring systems (basic)

3. SOFT SKILLS REQUIRED
- Communication and collaboration with international teams
- Problem-solving skills
- Time management and task prioritization
- Customer service orientation
- Documentation and tracking abilities

4. REALISTIC MATCH SCORE
40%

5. SKILL GAPS
- Prior experience (2+ years) in a help desk or IT support role
- Hands-on experience with Exchange Server Tools and Office 365 (cloud applications)
- Familiarity with building security and environment monitoring systems
- Experience working in a global/regional IT team environment
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or IT (if not held)

6. INTERVIEW TOPICS TO PREPARE
- Basic troubleshooting of hardware and software issues
- User account management and common network/email setups
- Understanding of Office 365 and cloud-based services
- Customer service scenarios in IT support
- Ticketing system workflows and prioritization
- Communication skills in a multicultural team environment

7. SHOULD I APPLY?
If you are an entry-level IT candidate transitioning into IT support and AI/automation roles, this job offers good exposure to a broad range of IT support tasks and international collaboration. However, the 2+ years experience and specific Exchange/Office 365 skills suggest it is more suited for someone with some prior help desk experience. If you have foundational IT skills and can quickly learn cloud tools, it is worth applying, especially if you can highlight any relevant internships or projects. Otherwise, consider gaining more hands-on help desk experience first.
